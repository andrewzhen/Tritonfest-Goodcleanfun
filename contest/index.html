Spring Triton Fest

Instagram photo contest rules

  • Must be a registered UCSD undergraduate or graduate student for at least one quarter this academic year (Fall '19, Winter '20, or Spring '20) to enter
  • One entry per Instagram account, the account holder must be the student to claim the prize
  • Can only win once during the whole contest regardless of prize tier
  • Judging criteria and prizes for each prompt will change from week-to-week. Refer to the respective Instagram post for judging criteria and prize details (@ucsdtritonfest)
